The Subscription Creep Problem

If you run a local business, trust, community centre, sports venue, or regional enterprise, take a quick look at your bank statements. How much are you sending overseas every month just to keep your digital doors open?

It's the “SaaS subscription creep” — and it quietly drains thousands of hard-earned dollars from regional budgets every single year.

  • $65+/month to Squarespace or Wix
  • $35+/month for Calendly
  • $45+/month for a premium form builder plugin
  • $50+/month for a basic customer manager

Stop renting your digital tools from overseas tech giants. Build your own asset instead. One cost, no subscriptions.
