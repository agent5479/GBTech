Pricing & Engagement

Every business is different — the billing model follows the job. Routine and specialist work is typically hourly; defined builds and ongoing care suit project quotes or retainers.

Hourly

Pay for the time the job takes — best for ad-hoc support, diagnostics, and open-ended specialist work.

  • Routine maintenance & user support — $117/hr
  • Specialist infrastructure & Marshall Solutions — $127/hr
  • Most routine fixes resolved same-day remotely
  • Emergency call-outs available for critical failures

Project-based

A fixed quote for a defined scope — cost and deliverables agreed before work starts.

  • Scoped quote with timeline and milestones
  • Suited to Marshall Solutions builds and infrastructure projects
  • One agreed price — no hourly creep on defined deliverables
  • Ideal when the outcome is clear but the build is substantial

Subscription maintenance

An ongoing retainer for predictable IT care — regular hours bundled at an agreed monthly rate.

  • Priority response & preventive maintenance
  • Monthly health reviews tailored to your environment
  • Covers routine systems maintenance & user support
  • Retainer sized to your needs — not a fixed off-the-shelf package
